<h3>Bhutan</h3>
<p id="info">
To apply visa for Bhutan the following criteria has to be fulfilled:
<ol>
	<li>Purchase of Bhutan tour packages.</li>
	<li>100% payment to be made before visa application.</li>
	<li>A legible scanned copy or digital photo of your passport.</li>
	<li>One digital PP size photo in formats of JPEG (.jpg or .jpeg) or PNG (.png) or BMP (.bmp) or GIF (.gif)</li>
</ol>
</p>
<p id="info">
Photo Requirements:
<ol>
	<li>Color or Grayscale.</li>
	<li>Recent photograph (not more than six months old).</li>
	<li>Shows you looking directly at the camera. No looking over one’s shoulder (portrait style).</li>
	<li>Shows eyes open and clearly visible. If you wear glasses there should be no flash reflection, no tinted lenses and the frames should not cover any part of your eyes.</li>
	<li>Sharp focus and clear – no shadows.</li>
	<li>Clean with no smudge marks.</li>
</ol>
<p id="info">
 (Note: Photo scanned from passport has smudge marks)
<br>
<br>
The process usual takes about 3 to 5 working days and is issued about 30 days prior to arrival in Bhutan.
</p>
