<h2>CULTURAL & HISTORICAL TOURS</h2>
<p>Cultural and Historical Tours are some of the best Bhutan tours designed for those craving to explore the depth of the Himalayan country and their historical highlights scattered in various parts of the nation. Your journey on a Bhutan tour will remind you of the true meaning of cultural authenticity and natural magnificence, which provides opportunities to enjoy the natural splendors, the friendly people and their lifestyle.  While on a cultural tour of this tiny Himalayan kingdom, you will visit Paro, Thimpu and Punakha and their cultural hubs, and the sublimely beautiful regions. Prayer flags fluttering on the hills, temples and dzongs (fortresses) dotted on the hilly landscapes, monasteries and various sacred sites will make your Bhutan holidays remarkable and an unforgettable experience. The prevalent historic Taktsang Monastery, also known as the Tiger’s Nest in Paro, is one of the most visited tourist places in Bhutan, which is perched on a hilltop. A historical tour in Bhutan may showcase the country’s magnificent architecture and arts and crafts which form a significant part of the Bhutanese identity.</p>

<h2>TREKKING & HIKING</h2>
<p>Trekking in Bhutan reveals a spectacular, unspoilt country steeped in ancient traditions – and with a history that is as tall as the Himalayas. Bhutan walking tours is the best way to experience its well-conserved pristine natural environment that passes through the villages of nomadic yak herders and showcases their closet rustic lifestyle. While some treks are short and suitable for beginners, others can be long and challenging. Hiking and trekking in Bhutan is truly an amazing experience that comprises walking through the tender and lush vegetations of the river valleys to the icy slopes of the eastern end of the Himalayas. The mountainous and magical Himalayan kingdom of Bhutan is a hiker’s paradise, which offers an opportunity to witness stunning views of unclimbed snow-capped mountains, ancient forest-grazed slopes dotted with historic fortress monasteries, temples and religious monuments. The most popular walking trails in Bhutan range from easy to moderate, and the most difficult high altitude treks such as Jumolhari Trek, Laya Gasa Trek, Druk Path Trek and Snowman Trek.</p>
